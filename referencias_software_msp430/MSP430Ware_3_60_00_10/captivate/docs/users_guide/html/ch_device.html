Device Family

CapTIvate™ is a touch sensing peripheral that is attached to a microcontroller. This chapter introduces the devices with CapTIvate™ technology on board.

The following device types are available:

  • Standard devices
  • Small form factor devices (Die Size / Chip Scale)
